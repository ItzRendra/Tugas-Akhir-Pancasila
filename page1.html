Who is Anies Baswedan?

Anies Rasyid Baswedan, born on 7 May 1969, is an Indonesian academic, activist, and politician who served as the Governor of Jakarta from 2017 to 2022. Prior to entering public service, he was a student activist and political analyst. Anies served as the Rector of Paramadina University before being appointed as the Minister of Education and Culture in the Joko Widodo administration. Additionally, he is the founder of Indonesia Mengajar, a program that selects, trains, and assigns university graduates for a one-year teaching mission across the country. Anies is the grandson of nationalist, journalist, and freedom fighter Abdurrahman Baswedan, and the cousin of Novel Baswedan. He is currently a candidate for the President of Indonesia in the 2024 election.

Who is Muhaimin Iskandar?

Abdul Muhaimin Iskandar (born 24 September 1966), commonly shortened to Muhaimin Iskandar, though also colloquially known as Cak Imin or Gus Imin, is an Indonesian politician, serving as a Deputy Speaker of the People's Representative Council since 2019, and the chairman of the National Awakening Party (PKB) since 2005. Previously, he served as Deputy Speaker of the People's Representative Council from 1999 until 2009, the Minister of Manpower from 2009 until 2014, and the Deputy Speaker of the People's Consultative Assembly from 2018 until 2019. He is running as a vice presidential candidate in the 2024 Indonesian presidential election, as the running mate of Anies Baswedan.
