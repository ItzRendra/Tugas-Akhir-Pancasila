02 - Prabowo Subianto dan Gibran Rakabuming

Who is Prabowo Subianto?

Prabowo Subianto Djojohadikusumo, born on 17 October 1951, is an Indonesian politician, businessman, and former army lieutenant general, currently serving as the Minister of Defense. Graduating from the Indonesian Military Academy in 1970, Prabowo served in the Special Forces (Kopassus) until his appointment as chief of the Strategic Reserve Command (Kostrad) in 1998. In the same year, he was dishonorably discharged from the military and subsequently banned from entering the United States due to alleged human rights violations. Prabowo has been characterized as a right-wing nationalist.

Who is Gibran Rakabuming?

Gibran Rakabuming Raka, born on 1 October 1987, is an Indonesian politician and businessman currently serving as the mayor of Surakarta ("Solo") in Central Java, Indonesia. As the eldest child of the seventh President of Indonesia, Joko Widodo, Gibran is actively involved in politics. In the upcoming 2024 Indonesian presidential election, he is running as the vice-presidential candidate alongside Prabowo Subianto.
